About us

This audio platform was created as a part of final year project, at Queen Mary, University of London. The objective of the platform was to integrate a new feature of the ‘tempo search’ into a eye pleasing and enjoyable interface. This would help users when they knew what types of songs they would want but not the specific song itself.

As the exciting new audio platform, TempoSearch lets people discover and enjoy a wide variety of music through a method of retrieval never done before. This platform aims to become known for its unique features, such as the interactive tempo search. Try it out!
