What features does the application have?

Normal Search

You can search for music normally, by entering the song name, artists, genre, or album. Once this is done all songs containing any information entered will be returned in a table.

Tempo Search

You can also search through tempo, using a songs bpm. This can be obtained in two ways, firstly manually, where a bpm is entered, or a bpm can be calculated through your interaction with the application. Songs will the desired tempo will be returned.

Playlists

Once the songs have been returned, and you are logged in you are able to create a playlist in which you can store your songs retrieved from any of the searches. This playlist can be edited if you want.
